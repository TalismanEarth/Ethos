# Ethos

Talisman Earth is an independent AI research lab utilizing computational data to solve critical challenges in planetary sustainability and cognitive preservation. Precise, well-commented, and purposeful.

** Our Ethos **

Talisman Earth was founded on the simple, uncompromising principle that technology should act as a caretaker, not an extractor. Born from a personal foundation of full-time caregiving, our lab applies that same level of profound dedication and meticulous attention to the planet and the human mind.

We do not operate on a traditional profit-driven model. Instead, we measure our success strictly by the positive externalities we generate. By utilizing open-source data, rigorous computational chemistry, and deep learning, we aim to solve critical challenges in sustainable infrastructure and cognitive preservation. We believe in open science, absolute privacy, and honest data.
